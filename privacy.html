Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

QB Agent is a desktop application that integrates with QuickBooks Online through the official Intuit API to generate accounting reports and assist with financial analysis.

Information We Access

When authorized by the user, the application may access accounting data from the user’s QuickBooks Online company through secure OAuth 2.0 authentication provided by Intuit.

This data may include:

  • Chart of accounts
  • Financial reports such as Profit & Loss and Balance Sheet
  • Transaction records necessary for report generation

How Data Is Used

The application processes QuickBooks data locally on the user's computer for the purpose of generating accounting reports and assisting with financial review.

No QuickBooks financial data is transmitted to external servers by this application.

Data Storage

OAuth authentication tokens required to access the QuickBooks API may be stored locally on the user's device to maintain connection to the QuickBooks account.

No financial data retrieved from QuickBooks is stored externally.

Third-Party Services

This application connects to QuickBooks Online using the official APIs provided by Intuit.

Use of QuickBooks Online is subject to Intuit’s own privacy policies and terms of service.

Security

The application uses OAuth 2.0 authentication provided by Intuit to securely connect to QuickBooks Online accounts.

Data Sharing

The application does not sell, distribute, or share accounting data with third parties.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

Contact

For questions regarding this privacy policy or the application, contact the application administrator.
